Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register?

No registration is required. Just configure your miner and start mining!

What is PPLNS?

PPLNS stands for "Pay Per Last N Shares". This reward system pays miners based on the last N shares contributed when a block is found.

When do I get paid?

Payments are processed automatically when your balance reaches the minimum payout threshold.
