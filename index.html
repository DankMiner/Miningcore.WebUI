Block Status Guide

  • New: Blocks just found, awaiting initial confirmation
  • Pending: Blocks with confirmations in progress
  • Confirmed: Fully confirmed blocks ready for payout
  • Orphaned: Blocks that were not included in the main chain
  • Uncle: Blocks that contribute partial rewards
Effort/Luck Indicator:
  • ● Green: Luck < 100% (Found faster than expected)
  • ● Orange: Luck 100-200% (Normal range)
  • ● Red: Luck > 200% (Took longer than expected)

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register?

No registration is required. Just configure your miner with your wallet address and start mining!

What is PPLNS?

PPLNS stands for "Pay Per Last N Shares". This reward system pays miners based on the last N shares contributed when a block is found. It rewards loyal miners and discourages pool hopping.

When do I get paid?

Payments are processed automatically when your balance reaches the minimum payout threshold. Check the pool configuration for specific amounts.

How accurate is the hashrate display?

The hashrate is calculated based on submitted shares. Short-term fluctuations are normal. The 3-hour average provides the most accurate representation.

What if my worker shows as offline?

Workers are marked offline if no shares are submitted for 15 minutes. Check your miner configuration and network connection.

Getting Started Guide

Quick Start

Follow these simple steps to start mining:

1. Choose Your Coin

Select a coin from the home page based on your hardware and preferences.

2. Get a Wallet

Download the official wallet for your chosen coin and generate a receiving address.

3. Download Mining Software

Choose compatible mining software for your hardware:

  • GPU Mining: T-Rex, NBMiner, TeamRedMiner
  • CPU Mining: XMRig, CPUminer
  • ASIC Mining: Check manufacturer's software

4. Configure Your Miner

Use the configuration examples from the Connect page for your specific setup.

miner.exe -a ALGORITHM -o stratum+tcp://POOL:PORT -u WALLET.WORKER

5. Monitor Your Stats

Enter your wallet address in the dashboard to view your mining statistics.

Troubleshooting

Low Hashrate

  • Ensure your GPU drivers are up to date
  • Check temperature and power limits
  • Try different mining software

Connection Issues

  • Verify firewall isn't blocking connections
  • Try alternative ports if available
  • Check your internet connection stability

No Shares Accepted

  • Confirm you're using the correct algorithm
  • Verify your wallet address is valid
  • Check if difficulty is appropriate for your hashrate
